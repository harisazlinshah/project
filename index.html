3 Steps For Healthy Eating

01

Eat fewer unhealthy fats such as fish and lean meats instead of fatty cuts of meat. Skip butter and lard, and use less margarine. Eat fewer high-fat dairy foods like cheese, ice cream, and whole milk.

02

Go light on salt by keeping the saltshaker off the table. Instead of adding salt when cooking, season your food with herbs and flavorings. Try lemon, garlic, and onion, or salt-free herb seasonings.

03

Pay attention to serving sizes. Remember that a serving size is a standard measurement. It will let you track the amount of fat, calories, and other nutrients in the food you eat. Read the Nutrition Facts label on packaged foods to learn their serving sizes.
